# Account Deletion & Data Management

We are committed to your data privacy. In accordance with Google Play's requirements, we provide a clear path for you to delete your account and any personal information we have collected.

## How to Request Deletion

Because we take security seriously, we require a verified request to ensure your data is not deleted by someone else. 

### Via Email (Web Request)
If you no longer have the app installed, please send an email from the address associated with your account:

* **To:** [hello.lifeassistant@gmail.com]
* **Subject:** Account Deletion Request - [Your Name/Username]
* **Body:** Please include a brief statement requesting the permanent removal of your account.

**Verification Step:** Once we receive your email, we will send a confirmation reply. You must reply to that confirmation to finalize the deletion process.

### In-App Deletion
If you still have the app installed, you can initiate this process instantly:
1. Open **[My Life Assistant]**.
2. Go to **Profile** > **Account**.
3. Select **Delete Account** and confirm.

---

## Data Scope & Retention
When you complete the deletion process, the following actions are taken:

* **Personal Identity:** We remove your name, email address, and any linked social media identifiers.
* **App Usage Data:** Any history, preferences, or user-generated content stored on our servers is purged.
* **Exemptions:** We may only retain data that we are legally obligated to keep for regulatory, tax, or fraud prevention purposes, as outlined in our primary Privacy Policy.

## Processing Time
Once verified, your account and data will be deleted from our active databases within **[7 business days]**, in-app this process is instant.
